Headline: Peter Obi demands Tinubu’s resignation over ‘monumental failure’ (Guardian Nigeria)

What happened: Peter Obi, the presidential candidate of the Nigerian Democratic Congress (NDC), has publicly called for President Bola Tinubu's resignation, citing "monumental failure" in governance. This demand comes amidst growing public discontent over the current administration's performance. Deep Lore / Context: Peter Obi was a prominent contender in the 2023 presidential election, representing the Labour Party, and garnered significant support, particularly among the youth. His challenge to President Tinubu's victory was ultimately unsuccessful in court.
